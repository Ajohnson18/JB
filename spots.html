Local Surf Spots

Many of the well known spots that local surfers love to ride during all seasons of the year. Some spots have been left out in order to keep their secrecy...

Bay Head

Bay Head is just south of Point Beach. Bay Head can offer some of the best tubes on the Jersey Shore. Bay Head has short jetties that create amazing waves during a hurricane swell. Parking in the summer at Bay Head is terrible so get ready to walk to the beach. Bay Head is a quiet and local town so the beach is not as crowded as in other places. Bay Head is definitely a local spot. Whenever the waves are good, at any time of the year. I'll definitely be in Bay Head.
